Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

Classic and timeless Caesar salad.

The perfect mix between healthy and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound boneless skinned chicken breast strips
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup seasoned croutons
  • ¼ cup creamy Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Cover 4 plates with lettuce.
  2. Top with all remaining ingredients except dressing.
  3. Drizzle with dressing just before serving.
